A Message From Our Founder

It is my belief that no person on this earth should have to look at a bald person. I created this to help ensure no one would ever have to look at a bald person again. With love, we are able to fund paper bags with cutouts for eyes for all bald people on earth. Eventually, if we are lucky, we hope to be able to completely destroy voluntary baldness.

Milestones

The first thing we fought for was a paper bag for each bald person on earth. No more would anyone have to be blinded by Brian's reflections.

Some baldies have even gone on to lead sucessful careers.
